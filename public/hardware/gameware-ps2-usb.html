Gameware PS2/USB Adaptor

Updated: 2008-05-29

Gameware seems to be Game's own-brand. There were no system requirements listed on the box, so I decided I could claim innocence if the product didn't work.

The device holds a single port for a single PS2 controller.

The adaptor seems to follow the USB HID inteface, so I just plugged it in (under Ubuntu 7.04), and changed the device configuration in ZSNES, and it just worked.

The analogue sticks also work fine.
