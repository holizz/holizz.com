Archive of 2005-01-31.

Guess what I was last night!

Stoned.

I suppose you’d like to know how a nice Straight Edge person like myself came to be in such a state of affairs. It would be a funny story if I didn’t hate the experience so much.

Okay, so Niall calls me at about 10pm or something last night (2005-01-30) reminding me about this party I was invited to. So I wander off to his halls. Somebody else is waiting at the door when I prank Niall to let me in. I later learn that she is called Jane (this has very little to do with anything; I’m just setting the scene). Amanda lets me and Jane in and we meet Niall on the stairwell (as the party was on the second floor). She mentions this “special vegan cake”. I assume its veganity is the only particularly special thing about it. FATAL MISTAKE NUMBER ONE.

So I’m offered a piece of cake and I accept. I eat some of it, it’s quite nice. Then I find out the other special feature of the cake: it has cannabis in it. At this point I am thinking “Oh shit…”. She said there was £35 of weed in it. I think that rather than forcing myself to throw up, it would be easier just to put up with it. After all, I didn’t eat that great a portion of the cake. FATAL MISTAKE NUMBER TWO.

So by the time I get home at about 2–3am, I am really stoned and I am really not enjoying it. I then write the following:

I’m not in control, even of my own body.

This is terrible, all my senses are blurred. I really don’t like it.

“I would normally go to bed and read” I was thinking. And now I can’t, because my mind’s all blurred. I wouldn’t understand it in this state. This is a terrible feeling. I’m having trouble typing, by Space! It’s not nice at all. Argh. I’m going to go to bed now, because it’s making me feel so odd.

At least I earned one thing from my experience, I now have a very good idea as to why I do not do drugs: I like to be in control of my own body.

I’m still slightly stoned, it’s not a nice feeling.

I believe I learned some valuble lessons:

  1. Check for undesireable ingredients other than eggs, milk and honey in cakes.
  2. DO NOT ACCEPT CAKES FROM STRANGERS ANYONE .
  3. Throwing up is better than pure hell.

Drugs are fantastic things to some people, but to me they are pure hell.
