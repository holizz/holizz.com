The Beginning

This is my new blog (old blog). Thank you, Niall, for the hosting.

So maybe sunc is full of cheap hacks and it doesn’t have comments (yet). It’s still better than Blogger, which makes it near-impossible to have good XHTML. It was just a case of “I can wait a few years for Blogger to implement something sensible or I can write my own”.

No comments, of course. So for now use email. My address is in the about page.

I could implement comments in five minutes (slight hyperbolation there) but really, they need some time to be implemented otherwise they’ll end up insecure and such. I expect I’ll use Gravatar with the comments too. It’ll use my Gravatar cache, which will reduce the strain on gravatar.com. And to be nice to Niall the gravatars will be served from my account on lamp.

The CMS has a nice DWIM thing to make sure it does what I mean. Like with my “quotes” and—dashes. It even has interrobangs (interrobangs‽). It’s great fun.
