Moving

Yesterday was spent moving from Bradford hall to Longside hall. It was relatively painless, I had my computer up and connected pretty much immediately (once I figured out the reason X wouldn’t start was because the mouse wasn’t plugged in—that will have to be fixed).

It was also the hottest day in the world ever!

Then I, Davina, and Barbara went to see The Cleaners at the Theatre in the Mill. It was very good, but they could do with some ventilation due to it being hot and people smoking on- and off-stage.

Today I was going to hand in my keys, swim, then lay on the amphitheatre in the shade and read. The reception was closed, there was too little chlorine; so I lay on the grass under a pine tree with pine cones and learned some irregular verbs (io ho, tu hai, lei ha, etc.) and read Feed.
