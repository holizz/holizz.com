Questionnaire

This questionnaire was received via email, from Niall.

What time did you get up this morning?
Just before half past nine. I was scared I’d turned off my alarm and then forgotten about it, but I woke up and I was up before my alarm. Early nights are great.
Diamonds or pearls?
Diamonds because they’re not an animal product.
What was the last film you saw at the cinema?
Sin City. About an hour ago.
What is your favourite TV show at the moment?
At the moment the only TV show I’m watching is Sliders and I only have one episode left before I run out.
What did you have for breakfast?
I missed breakfast due to swimming and then having to run off to Thornton.
What is your middle name?
There’s a flawed assumption in that question.
What is your favourite cuisine?
I don’t have one. I love a great many foods.
What do you dislike?
Now there’s an essay question.
What is your favourite crisp flavour?
I rarely eat crisps.
What is your favourite CD at the moment?
I’m quite happy with the couple of FreeBSD CDs I burnt the other day and subsequently used to install FreeBSD.
What kind of car do you drive?
The kind that doesn’t pollute, doesn’t have the potential to kill if my concentration slips for a second. In other words, I don’t drive.
Favourite sandwich?
Peanut butter, jam, cashew nuts. I ran out of cashew nuts yesterday.
What characteristic do you despise?
Selfishness.
Favourite item of clothing?
I do love my boots very much.
If you could go anywhere in the world on holiday where would you go?
I would like to visit Italy when I speak Italian semi-fluently.
What colour is your bathroom?
White, probably.
Favourite brand of clothing?
Pink Pineapple. But they don’t make clothing any more. I don’t even know if it’s still going.
Where would you retire to?
Who says I’m going to retire?
Favourite time of the day?
Who knows? I like crazy times when nobody’s awake.
What was your most memorable birthday?
My 17th and 18th were both very memorable.
Where were you born?
Bradford Royal Infirmary.
Favourite sport to watch?
I don’t watch sport except when I rest my eyes and there are people playing in the amphitheatre.
Person you expect to send it back first?
Please. Cut the clichés.
Coke or Pepsi?
I despise Coke more but I’d drink neither.
Are you a morning or a night person?
I used to be a night person, but I’m becoming a morning person thanks to swimming.
What is your shoe size?
My boots say 11, my sandals say 12.
Do you have any pets?
No.
Any new and exciting news you’d like to share with your friends?
No.
What did you want to be when you were little?
At one point I wanted to be an aerodynamic engineer.
