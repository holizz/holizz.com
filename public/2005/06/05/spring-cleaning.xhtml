Spring Cleaning

Yesterday I was bored so I decided to clean up a bit. Then I found (dum dum DUM) carpet beetle larvae. So I vacuumed them all up. In the end I managed to vacuum the whole side of the room they were on. So now under my computer it’s all clean and the wires almost look lonely. It’s also very clean under my bed (they were mostly under my bed, I think they came in from under the carpet in the corner). There’s still a whole load of dead ones, mostly under my bed. I don’t think they’ll be coming out of the carpet. I also cleaned out my cupboard in the kitchen, I still have to wash up and such, but that can wait.

Today I am going to clean the other side of the room and wash all the clothes I’d left there. I doubt there’ll be much in the way of carpet beetle larvae on that side but it’s best to clean it anyway, to make sure the ones I missed don’t go make a new home over there.

As far as housekeeping goes, I’ve learnt my lesson.
