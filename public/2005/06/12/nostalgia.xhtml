Nostalgia

Well, I’m just doing some last-minute packing (I’m taking some stuff to Davina’s tomorrow so I don’t have to take all my stuff to Longside when I move there next Saturday), and the nostalgia got the better of me after looking inside my pencil case from last year and then looking at my current calendar and seeing how little there is on it (about of a third of the dates marked are exams, of which I had three) compared to my 2004 calendar.

Whenever I do any amount of moving of my stuff (the older the better!) I get nostalgic, and often I miss living with Davina. I like living on my own, but it’s just when I recall living with Davina that I miss it. Maybe I’m just too distracted all the time to think about it.

I also feel guilty about not reading or using things Davina gives me. Like this empty 2004–2005 diary, or all those animal rights booklets and magazines she’s given me.

Yup, I’m done for tonight. I have plenty to fill Davina’s car with. I also have five hours of sleep (I think she’s coming round at 10, but I can’t check now).
