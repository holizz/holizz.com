Late

I woke up and thought I didn’t set my alarm, what time is it?. Somehow it was 07:30 (half an hour before I was planning on waking up), so I set my alarm for 08:00. Seems like I woke up, turned it off, and went back to sleep because I woke up three minutes before my first lecture when Will texted me to see if I was going. Needless to say, I am not; I am here journalling.

I’m very sorry, it was 12 days since my last post and lots of things have happened. Well, not that many. But some important things.

  • I started using the Dvorak layout Wednesday (I think) last week and I’m still using it. It’s a great layout.
  • My Happy Hacker Lite 2 keyboard arrived on Saturday, from the Linux Emporium. It’s very small and very cool.
  • Star Wars: Revelations was released yesterday; Star Wars: Revelations Available Online. It was rather fantastic.
  • Google Maps UK was launched today. See Google does Grimsby, Gateshead and Glasgow.
