Things I’ve been meaning to do

It started at Rios, like most Saturday mornings. I spent much more time dancing than I usually do. I danced to almost every song in the goth room until I started to get tired.

I did lots of things I’ve been meaning to do for a while today.

  1. First of all I shifted my curtains round to one side so I could get lots of sunlight after deciding it was the best source of vitamin D.
  2. Then I did something with the yellow split-peas Sammy gave me: I stuck them in a pan with cashew nuts and made dhal.
  3. Then I played Metroid Prime for the first time in months. I started a new game and played up till the first save to get re-acquainted with the controls. I expect I’ll play from where I left off later on, maybe tomorrow.
  4. I had laundry day! For the first time in, well, too long.
  5. I read the RELAX NG Tutorial. It looks very interesting but I’m left wondering how one would go about associating a document with a schema for validation.

It’s been an excellent day. I’m not sure why but I’m in a fantastic mood.
