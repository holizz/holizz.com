Worst. Birthday. Ever.

Comparatively speaking, of course. But it sucks compared to most days I’ve had recently. I expect it’s because I was excited about it (not that I’d shown it—in fact I tried to keep it hidden from myself).

Last birthday began at Rios, just like this birthday. But last year I went to see Damien Rice and two days after my birthday I had a party thrown for me. It was very exciting.

The birthday before that also began at Rios. But that birthday I was fucked on ecstasy and I stayed up all night with friend a and friend b sitting on my bed talking and then in the morning David, Mandy, Rebecca and Tory came round and we had lunch at South Square Café and all the time I was thinking “I’m so fucked and I can’t tell them”. It was very exciting.

This birthday was, of course, welcomed in while dancing at Rios. But the difference is that I have nothing else planned for today or any time soon. Sure I opened nice presents but nothing happened. It was just a let down. Somewhere in my mind was saying “oh this is going to be so fun‼” but a more wizened and conscious area was saying “on no it fucking won’t”.

So this year I’m not seeing musicians, partying or taking recreational drugs. I’m sitting at home eating burnt rice/soup with under-cooked potatoes.

I suppose the day hasn’t ended up badly, at least. I read XHTML 1.1 - Module-based XHTML and I’m currently reading a copy of The Principia Discordia .
