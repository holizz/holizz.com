Meme

Another meme from Neil.

  2. Diamonds or pearls? Diamonds. They’re not animal products.
  3. What was the last film you saw at the cinema? Team America—World Police.
  4. What is your favourite TV show? I’d have to say ER.
  5. What did you have for breakfast? I’m eating it now. Morrisons’ Malties with expensive “organic” soy milk.
  6. What is your middle name? My middles names are Malcolm and John.
  7. What is your favourite cuisine? Middle Asian. I love curries.
  8. What foods do you dislike? Mushrooms and bell peppers.
  9. What is your favourite crisp/chip flavour? Ready salted is good. But I prefer peanuts.
  10. What is your favourite CD at the moment? Sarge/Testing installation disk one. I think it’s the CD that was in my DVD-ROM drive last.
  11. What kind of vehicle do you drive? I don’t drive. I was learning but I gave it up because it was too much effort.
  12. Favourite sandwich? I’ll have to say an alfalfa + etc. sandwich.
  13. What characteristics do you despise? Heh, I almost read that as characters (in which case it would have been Microsoft’s proprietary quotation marks it uses instead of actual Unicode quotation marks). Meanness covers a lot. A nice, terse term.
  14. Favourite item of clothing? My sexy coat.

  17. What colour pants are you wearing? Black.

  19. Favourite time of the day? Some time in the evening.
  20. What was your most memorable birthday? 17th.

  22. Whats the last thing you ate? My breakfast. See above.

  24. Favourite flower? Self-raising.

  49. Full names of your potential kids? My potential kids do not have names as they’re only potential. Idiot.
  50. Usual bedtime? It ranges from around 0100 till 0500. It all depends on what I’m doing in the evening. Sometimes I have to tear myself away from what I’m doing such that I only wake up an hour after my alarm goes off.
