Pancakes, an explosion and miscellany

Today (yes, two whole days after pancake day) I had some pancakes. They turned out reasonably well. One looked like a Bird of Prey.

The small explosion came when I put a little too much oil in the pan. I tipped it out into a cup and some touched the ring. The result was a small bang as the oil exploded. This is precisely why I am not planning on deep-fat frying anything any time soon (also it’s against the rules in halls).

I just updated to the latest Sarge/testing version of Mozilla Thunderbird (1.0-3). In accordance with Mozilla’s trademark on Mozilla, it has now been renamed Debian Thunderbird (since third-party builds cannot be called Mozilla Firefox). I think Mozilla Firefox will have to be renamed too.
