Busy days

Looks like somebody sold my name, address and date of birth. I have a “happy birthday” invitation to Reflex. I’m not happy. I’m going to have to start using spam names when I subscribe to postal things. Like using company_name@this_domain or holizz+company_name@gmail.com as it used to be.

Cat told me off because my blog didn’t work in IE tonight. I told her it’s IE’s fault but I’m working on it. Damn bloody IE.

I hate being so busy. Thursdays aren’t usually that bad, but today was; I did two hours of lectures, laundry day which lasted far too long (we got back and mine hadn’t even started) then I had to quickly make and eat pasta and go to LGBT (I signed something for a Union boycott of the National Blood Service and a thing to nominate Fran again) then I ate pancakes, blogged and ran off to Morrisons to see Davina and then go to the pub. Fridays are the worst though, five hours of lectures then I usually go to Thornton to babysit (but not this week) then I usually go out to Rios (it’s Kulu this week … which finishes at 6am. No I will not be staying till the end).

So much to do and so little time to do it all! So busy! I can’t stand being so busy; having no time to sit down and relax, browse blogs, blog, read!
