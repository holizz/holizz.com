Orbitz is silly

Slashdot reports that Orbitz has a very interesting new set of Terms & Conditions.

There are amusing statements like the following:

For your convenience, our Site provides links to other sites. When you click on one of these links, you are leaving our site and entering another site.

URI: http://www.orbitz.com/global/legal/terms.jsp?type=termsNew#p6

But there are also some very odd requests they make which will never work:

We reserve the right to require you to remove links to the Site, in our sole discretion.

Linking to any page of the Site other than to the homepage is strictly prohibited in the absence of a separate linking agreement with Orbitz.

URI: http://www.orbitz.com/global/legal/terms.jsp?type=termsNew#p6

I will link to what I like. They can suck it (oh dear, I think I picked “suck it” up from Dooce).

Personally, I don’t think these ridiculous terms and conditions I’ve seen recently could be enforced in the slightest. I generally don’t read terms and conditions even if I notice the little text at the bottom of the page. So how these extreme terms and conditions could possibly be applicable to anybody is beyond me.
