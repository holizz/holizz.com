DJs Phil and Emma

If, Space-forbid, you are unfamiliar with DJs Phil and Emma: they DJ on a Wednesday night at Bradford Rio. Then often play terrible music. I often request S-Club 7 as it’s better than a lot of the shit they play.

I was in the Courtyard bar in the Communal Building with Niall, Wendy, Mike, and Amanda. Stuart had just left. And what does Amanda say? I don’t know; I didn’t quite hear. “Did you just say you knew the Wednesday night DJ at Rios?” I inquire. As it turns out her boyfriend shares an abode with DJ Phil and Emma.

She then went on to mention somebody who always requests S-Club 7 on a Wednesday. Wow. Phil/Emma has noticed me. Summer was mentioned too. Apparently they want to slap us.

I only joked about it before. But we really are the celebrities of Rios.

The other Friday DJ Simon pushed Summer and stood on my foot, which either means he’s very clumsy or he hates us. So maybe everybody does hate us. I do hope so.

Oh yeah, we got the results for KSCP1 (the formal report—ours was called The Internet: Beyond the Porn). Our group (consisting of myself, Stuart, Will and John) got 14/20, not bad. I’ll upload a copy once I decide on a URI for it.
