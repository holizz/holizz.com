Blogs past and present

I’ve had four URIs for blogs/journals in the past. There is, of course http://holizz.com/. Some of you may remember http://holizz.niallfleming.ath.cx/ (which redirects here because I redirected /year/month/etc. and I couldn’t be bothered spending a while getting / to leave it be—but that’s okay, all the content from there is here).

But before that, what was there? Of course! http://holizz.blogspot.com/! That lasted from 2004-08-09 till 2005-01-11 when it was obsoleted by http://holizz.niallfleming.ath.cx/. That wasn’t too long now, was it? Only 70 entries.

And before that? Does anybody remember?

Why http://www.deadjournal.com/~holizz/, of course! That lasted from 2002-11-17 till 2003-10-27. I eventually got bored of it and left it in the capable hands of Summer (because deadjournal is invitation-only). So I’ve been blogging (back in my day it was called a journal ;) for two years and three months!

And what brought this nostalgia on, you ask? Why I’m doing my Blogger Code.

I think I need to create a Web crawler to trawl deadjournal and blogger to get all my entries back. Then I can stick them into my current CMS and have an archive spanning over two years of my life. I think it would be a good idea. It may or may not be easier to simply mirror each site so that the bot doesn’t take hours to run.

B7 d t++ k s u-- f i o+ x e l++ c--

You can use this to decode it.
