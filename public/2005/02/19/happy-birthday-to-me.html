Happy birthday to me

I’m 19 today. As with my last two birthdays and various pseudo-important events, the date clicked over while at Rios. Dancing to a song I like.

It was an enjoyable night. I was dancing for most of it. With my lovely friend Summer.

After Summer left at around 1am an odd fellow from Seattle sat next to me and talked absolute shit to me in his horrible accent. The topic ranged from the “booger” I have in my nose to his Chevy (the stupid American prick) to his interest in Summer’s arse. He actually used the phrase “Life’s a bitch” and in his horrible accent it was very hard to keep myself from laughing in his face.

If you’re out there, prick from Seattle with a Chevy, hello!

So what exciting things have I done today? I opened some presents and made breakfast and lunch. Nothing magical.

But surely you have some exciting thing to do tonight, o great Holizz‽

I was going to The Wendy House but that didn’t work out. I’ll probably just read Practical RDF (it’s one of my presents from Davina) and try and make my CMS generate indices. I may even cook something nice.

I was quite surprised to get £20 of book tokens from South Square Centre. That was nice of them.

And for those who are wondering, urn:isbn:0-596-00263-7 is a perfectly valid URI, it’s just that most browsers don’t do anything with it. Yet.
