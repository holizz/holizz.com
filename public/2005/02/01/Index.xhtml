Saturday Q and a new term

Yes, I did Friday Q on Saturday (heresy!). But then I forgot about it.

I just watched Team America—World Police with Stuart, Niall, Mike, Laura, and Phil. I remembered all their names, I rule. It was pretty amusing.

I almost felt as if I’d stepped onto the tips of the hairs of the rabbit being pulled from the hat of the universe, staring into the eyes of the illusionist just now when I thought of how I was last night. I was just concentrating on getting to sleep as soon as possible so I could sleep off the nightmare. I even put myself in the recovery position before I fell asleep (or as close as memory serves).

There’s nothing like a nightmarish experience to make one happy to be alive and sober.

Today I went to Introduction to Web Technologies. It was rather boring. Then it was Software Development (Programming) in the same room, that was boring too. Then I went home and slept, thus missing Formal Foundations, which I hear is good. About strings, they say. I hear exciting things will be taught. Then I slept again till just before the film, then I watched the film and came home. Now I shall leave you with my mostly-complete Friday Q… from Saturday.

FQ TOPIC: Tip.

It’s Friday Q… but I’m doing it on a Saturday. Please don’t eat me.

FQ1: Share a health or beauty tip!

Eat well. Listen to your body. Drugs are a last resort.

FQ2: Share a computer or electronic gadget tip! FQ3: Share a travel or transportation tip!

Always keep paper and a pen near by. Or equivalent. I keep my notepad and a pen with me all the time. You never know when you might come up with a great idea to implement later in code.

FQ TIPSTER: Got a tip for a classic or vintage CD, movie, TV show, or book we might have overlooked?

Isaac Asimov’s Foundation series. But they’re at the end of the Foundation universe. I’m planning on reading them in order.
