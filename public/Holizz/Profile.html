When did you get into GNU/Linux?
I was 17 when I discovered GNU/Linux. SuSE 9.0 arrived the day I got my nipples pierced.
How did you get into GNU/Linux?
I think I may have noticed it in connection with Mozilla, which I had recently downloaded and I started using that instead of IE. Then slowly my interest grew and I read articles by RMS explaining why Free Software was necessary and why it was a good idea. His philosophies matched my own pretty closely. So I tried this Freedom thing in the form of SuSE 9.0 (yes, I realise that YAST wasn’t free in 9.0, YAST was the reason I switched away from SuSE) and I enjoyed it (and accidentally wiped Windows). So I stuck with it. And I had no Internet access for a week because I had a software modem. But I still loved it.
Are you a GNU/Linux zealot?
No, I love all Free (as in software) OSes (although I have very little experience with anything other than GNU/Linux).
